Religare Retail Research report on HDFC Bank

HDFC Bank’s net interest income reported marginal increase of 2.1% QoQ to Rs 29,077cr as margins remained flat. Pre-provision operating profit (PPOP) grew by 23.8% QoQ to Rs 29,274cr, however, PAT remained flat with a growth of 0.9% QoQ as provisions increased by 220% QoQ. Other income grew by 63.1% QoQ mainly due to higher treasury income and gain from stake sale in HDFC Credelia Financial Services.
